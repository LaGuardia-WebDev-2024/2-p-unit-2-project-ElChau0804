Places to go in Shanghai!

The Bund

The bund is a sightseeing spot, known for its night light up show and being a waterfront, visited by many. There is a mix of historical architecture and modern skyscrapers side by side on pudong.


The Yuyuan garden

A beautiful historical traditional garden, where you can walk through and see classical art, architecture, flowers and more.



Biggest Popmart

Shanghai holds one of the worlds biggest popmart buildings, with so many different figures on display like the Nyota, Hirono, Skull panda and so many others.
