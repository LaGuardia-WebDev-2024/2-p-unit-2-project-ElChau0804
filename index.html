Places to go in Shanghai!

The Bund

The bund is a sightseeing spot, known for its night light up show and being a waterfront, visited by many. There is a mix of historical architecture and modern skyscrapers side by side on pudong.
Between the bund is an underwater transportation system as well where you can see the water through a beautiful clear tunnel with lights, connecting Pudong and Puxi!



The Yuyuan garden

A beautiful historical traditional garden, where you can walk through and see classical art, architecture, ponds, dragon walls and more.
It is separated into multiple scenic halls, built to provide a peaceful place for Pan's (a government official of the ming dynastys) parents.



Biggest Popmart

Shanghai holds one of the worlds biggest popmart buildings, with so many different figures on display like the Nyota, Hirono, Skull panda and so many others.
They have limited edition figures, a hirono jewelry store(idk if its in this store but there is one in Shanghai), and beautiful popmart architecture in the mall its in!
